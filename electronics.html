SERVO CONTROL

This art project was a collaboration with a close friend of mine, as a statement of the current state of management and far-right tendencies of the Slovak government. Using PWM pins of arduino to drive servo motors, we are controlling airflow to produce sound by blowing on half-empty beer bottles. The music being played is a botched-up version of Slovak National Anthem.
